# Supply Chain Business Insights Report

## Executive Summary

This project analyzes supply chain operations using Python, SQL, Excel, and Power BI. The primary objective is to identify business opportunities, improve operational efficiency, optimize inventory levels, monitor supplier performance, reduce delivery delays, and increase overall profitability.

The analysis covers order transactions, product performance, supplier contribution, warehouse utilization, inventory management, shipping costs, and fulfillment performance.

---

# Business Objectives

The key business objectives are:

- Improve supply chain visibility.
- Increase revenue and profitability.
- Optimize inventory levels.
- Reduce delivery delays.
- Improve warehouse efficiency.
- Evaluate supplier performance.
- Minimize shipping costs.
- Increase customer satisfaction.

# Revenue Analysis

Revenue is one of the most important business KPIs.

Revenue was calculated using:

Revenue = Quantity × Unit Price

Key objectives of revenue analysis:

- Identify top-performing products.
- Identify high-value suppliers.
- Measure warehouse contribution.
- Track monthly revenue trends.
- Detect revenue concentration risk.

### Business Value

Revenue analysis helps management understand:

- Which products generate maximum profit.
- Which suppliers contribute most revenue.
- Which warehouses handle highest business volume.

---

# Product Performance Analysis

Products contribute differently to business growth.

The analysis includes:

- Top-performing products.
- Lowest-performing products.
- Product-wise revenue contribution.
- Product sales distribution.

### Key Insights

- A small number of products usually generate a majority of revenue.
- Underperforming products increase inventory holding costs.
- Product demand patterns can guide procurement planning.

### Recommendations

- Focus marketing on top products.
- Reduce excess stock for low-demand products.
- Monitor product lifecycle regularly.
